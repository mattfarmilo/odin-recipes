Potato & Bacon Soup

Description

A delicious winter treat

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable stock paste
  • 1 tbsp chicken stock powder
  • 200g bacon
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 sticks celery
  • salt, to taste
  • black pepper, to taste
  • 600g potato
  • 950g water (use less for a thicker soup)

Steps

  1. Place celery & bacon into Thermomix. Turbo 3 seconds.
  2. Add both stocks, butter, 100g water, salt and pepper. Speed 3, 100c, 3 mins.
  3. Add 750g water and potato. Speed 1, 100c, 20 mins.
  4. Blend 10 seconds, speed 6
