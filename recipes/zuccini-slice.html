Zuccini Slice

Description

A low FODMAP zuccini slice, perfect for hiding vegetables from fussy toddlers. Tasty, easy to make and a great way to use up whatever vegetables need to be used.

Ingredients

  • 2 zuccini's
  • extra vegetables
  • 4 rashers bacon
  • 1 1/2 cups grated cheese
  • 1/2 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup cornflour
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 6 eggs, lightly beaten

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 170c
  2. Add zuccini, extra vegetables and bacon to Thermomix. Chop on speed 5 for a few seconds
  3. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and mix well
  4. Pour into a baking dish and bake for 30-40 minutes or until firm and golden
  5. Allow to stand for 5 minutes before serving
