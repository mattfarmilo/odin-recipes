Scalloped Potatoes

Description

Yum, yum, yum!!

Ingredients

  • 42g butter
  • 4 tsp crushed garlic
  • 1/4 cup plain flour
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp dried thyme, rosemary, oregano etc
  • 8 medim potatoes, peeled & thinly sliced
  • 2-3 cups cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200c
  2. Lightly grease a roasting dish approx 33 x 23cm
  3. Have all sauce ingredients ready as cooking times are fast
  4. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat
  5. Add garlic and simmer for 1 min
  6. Add flour and stir well. Cook gently for a further 1-2 minutes, ensuring it does not burn
  7. Add chicken stock and whisk until there are no lumps. It will look quite thick
  8. Add milk, salt, pepper and herbs. Whisk until fully combined
  9. Slowly heat the sauce until it just begins to simmer at the edges and slightly thicken (approx 2-3 minutes). Remove from heat and set aside
  10. Add half the potatoes to the dish, then half the cheese and half the sauce
  11. Repeat with the remaining potatoes, cheese and sauce
  12. Cover dish with aluminium foil and bake for 30 minutes
  13. Remove foil and bake for a further 30-40 minutes, until potatoes are cooked through
  14. Remove from the oven and allow to sit for 15 minutes for the sauce to thicken before serving
